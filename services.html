OUR CAPABILITIES

Services & Operations

Surgical, open-book trades coordination executing architectural framing, high-end finishing, and weatherproof cladding.

Capabilities ∨
01

Framing & Structural Backbones

We frame residences to stand for generations. Utilizing engineered wood products (LVLs), custom timber framing, and rigid load-path trusses, our assemblies ensure plumb walls, flat ceilings, and square corners as the foundation for premium finishes.

  • Engineered LVL beams & joist layouts
  • Precision shear-wall and load-bearing designs
  • Strict deflection tolerances exceeding code minimums
02

Luxury Custom Renovations

Reconstructing older spaces to fit modern architectural blueprints requires surgical precision. We coordinate span expansions, install structural steel load columns, and build seamless floor plan integrations.

  • Open-concept conversions & load-bearing wall removals
  • Bespoke kitchens & master suite layouts
  • Seamless historical additions and expansions
03

Level 5 Drywall & Taping

True quality is visible in the smoothness of your walls. We skim-coat surfaces to a Level 5 finish, utilize dustless vacuum sanding systems, and inspect all taping under high-angle raking lights.

  • Full skim-coating for smooth, non-textured paint surfaces
  • Bespoke plaster reveals & shadowline details
  • Acoustic isolation panels for sound mitigation
04

Smart Electrical & Automation

Future-ready power infrastructure. We integrate smart home control panels, design architectural recessed lighting layout grids, install structured low-voltage data feeds, and wire high-capacity EV charging stations.

  • Lutron whole-home automated lighting controls
  • EV charging stations and solar-ready panel pre-wiring
  • Art gallery and high-end residential lighting design
05

High-Performance Plumbing & Hydronics

Bespoke water distribution networks designed for maximum efficiency. We install custom copper and PEX distribution manifolds, multi-zone hydronic in-floor radiant heating loops, and high-efficiency tankless water heaters.

  • Wall-mounted European fixtures & rough-ins
  • Multi-zone radiant floor heating system setups
  • Acoustic sewer drop wrapping for soundproof utility lines
06

Weatherproof Siding & Envelopes

Protect your residence from harsh Alberta weather. We build rainscreen cladding assemblies and install premium James Hardie siding, composite wood panels, and continuous exterior thermal insulation wrappers.

  • James Hardie fiber cement board & batten layout
  • Modern metal cladding and rainscreen systems
  • High R-value continuous insulation envelopes
